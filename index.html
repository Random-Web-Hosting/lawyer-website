About Patrick Law Firm

Every Client Matters

If you are under an investigation or have been charged with a crime, you recognize the need to have someone on your side protecting your rights and vigorously providing your defense. At Patrick Law Firm, you will find the quality legal representation you need to protect your freedom.

Our practice focuses on all misdemeanor and felony criminal matters in the state of California, including DUI, domestic violence, drug charges, theft cases, and more serious criminal offenses. You will have the best criminal defense team fighting for you until the very end at all levels of the criminal justice system.

In Patrick Law Firm, you will have a defense attorney with extensive courtroom experience who takes the time to understand your unique circumstances and tip the scales of justice in your favor.

Our Clients Win

Over the last 10 years 98% of our clients have recovered compensation for their pain, suffering and financial losses. That is true for automobile and bicycle accidents, medical malpractice, Kaiser claims, brain injury and paralysis, wrongful death, defective products, and every kind of case we handle.

Most awarded

We have been selected by US News as among the “Best Law Firms in America”. Every one of our partners has been selected by “Best Lawyers”. All rated Walkup lawyers hold the highest ratings for professionalism and ethics from the Martindale-Hubbell rating agency. 10 of our lawyers have been listed by SuperLawyers; five have been nominated for the San Francisco Trial Layers of the Year Award - one of them four different times; one is the 2015 San Francisco Personal Injury “Lawyer of the Year” as determined by US News Best Lawyers; our lawyers have been honored by local and state bar associations, national honor societies and state organizations.

No upfront fees

We are a 100% contingency fee firm. We charge no upfront fees or retainers for investigation or expert reviews. We advance the money to investigate, file, prosecute and where necessary, try our client’s cases. We are only paid when we recover at the end of the case. No recovery, no fee.

We beat insurance companies

Since 1959 we have fought against insurance companies for our clients. Auto insurers, homeowners insurers, property insurers, medical insurers, casualty, excess, umbrella, special Ines and city, county and government insurers know we will fight for our clients. Insurance companies are not an injured person’s friend - we know that and fight for your rights.

Clients like us

We are the #1 rated San Francisco personal injury firm on YELP. Our YELP reviewers consistently refer to our concern for them as human beings and our professionalism. We care about our clients as people.

Over $480 million recovered

We Get Results. Through the years we have recovered more than $480 million dollars on behalf of clients injured through the wrongful conduct of others. We have prevailed and recovered money for clients injured in a multitude of circumstances.

We try cases

While most cases settle, some cases need to be tried for our clients to recover full value. Insurance companies and major corporations will not pay full value of a case if they are not afraid of the plaintiff’s lawyer in trial. If your lawyers cannot win in trial you have no negotiating power. We win in trial; judges and defense lawyers know that. Our team of lawyers have been honored as “Trial Lawyer of the Year” by the San Francisco Trial Lawyers Association and the California Chapter of the American Board of Trial Advocates.

Doctor on staff

Unlike 99% of other firms we have a full time physician attorney on our staff and a second doctor-lawyer available on call to consult on medical questions, expert finding and medical malpractice case screening. These skilled professionals help us understand our clients’ medical issues and select the best testifying medical professionals for deposition and trial.
